- **Formula**: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers √ó 100

- **Formula**: (New Followers - Lost Followers) / Total Followers √ó 100

- **Formula**: Clicks / Impressions √ó 100
- **Target**: 2-5% for promotional posts, 1-3% for educational

- **Formula**: Shares / Total Engagements √ó 100

- Identify top-performing hashtags

## 14. Legal & Compliance

### Disclosure Requirements

**Sponsored Content:**
- All paid partnerships must include #ad or #sponsored
- Must be clearly visible (not hidden in hashtag list)
- Follows FTC Endorsement Guidelines

**Affiliate Links:**
- Disclose if links earn commission
- Use "Affiliate link" or "I may earn from this link"

**User Testimonials:**
- Must be real, verifiable users
- Cannot fabricate or incentivize fake reviews
- Disclose if user received compensation

### Copyright Guidelines

**Original Content:**
- GIG3 owns rights to all branded content created in-house
- Freelancers/agencies retain portfolio rights with proper credit

**User-Generated Content:**
- Always get explicit permission before reposting
- Respect creator ownership and credit
- Cannot use UGC in paid advertising without written consent

**Third-Party Content:**
- Never repost without permission
- Use licensed stock photos only
- Attribute sources for data/statistics

### Crypto Advertising Regulations

**Platform-Specific Rules:**
- **Twitter/X**: Generally permissive for crypto content
- **Facebook/Instagram**: Restrictions on crypto ads (may require special approval)
- **Google Ads**: Crypto advertising allowed with certification
- **TikTok**: Limited crypto advertising, check current policy

**Best Practices:**
- Never make price predictions or financial advice
- Avoid "get rich quick" language
- Disclose risks when discussing crypto payments
- Comply with local securities laws

**Restricted Claims:**
- Don't guarantee earnings or returns
- Don't claim "no risk" or "100% safe"
- Don't encourage impulsive financial decisions

### Platform-Specific Rules

**Twitter/X:**
- Follow Twitter Rules (no spam, manipulation)
- Avoid excessive tagging
- No buying followers or engagement

**Instagram:**
- Follow Community Guidelines
- No misleading tags or locations
- Respect intellectual property

**LinkedIn:**
- Professional conduct required
- No overt sales pitches in comments/DMs
- Respect connection's feed (don't overshare)

**Discord:**
- Follow Discord Terms of Service
- Moderate for spam and abuse
- No DM marketing without permission
